About Us

AQUADIVE NIGERIA LIMITED is an indigenous diving and marine services company registered under Nigerian law. We specialize in providing commercial diving, underwater engineering, inspection, repair, and marine support services to the oil & gas, shipping, and offshore construction industries. With a commitment to safety, professionalism, and efficiency, AQUADIVE combines local expertise with global industry standards (IMCA, ADCI, and NUPRC guidelines) to deliver reliable underwater solutions that meet and exceed client expectations.

To deliver safe, cost-effective, and high-quality underwater services. To build strong partnerships with clients through trust and performance. To contribute to Nigeria’s local content development in the offshore industry.
